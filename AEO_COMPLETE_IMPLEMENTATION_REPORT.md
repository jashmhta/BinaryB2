## What is AEO?

**Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)** is the practice of optimizing content to be easily understood, extracted, and cited by AI-powered search engines and chatbots. Unlike traditional SEO which focuses on ranking in search results, AEO focuses on being the source of direct answers provided by AI systems.

### Key Differences: SEO vs AEO

| Aspect | Traditional SEO | Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) |
|--------|----------------|----------------------------------|
| **Goal** | Rank high in search results | Be cited as the answer source |
| **Format** | Keywords, backlinks, rankings | Conversational, direct answers |
| **Audience** | Search engine algorithms | AI language models |
| **Content Style** | Keyword-optimized | Natural language, comprehensive |
| **Measurement** | Rankings, traffic | Citations, answer accuracy |
| **Optimization** | Meta tags, links | Structured data, entity clarity |
